This plugin helps you make money from your CE-based community site by allowing you to charge members for premium content or features. It's currently in use on TennisMetro.com and Curbly.com.

Requires CommunityEngine version 1.2.0 or greater

Features

  • Authentication using Facebook credentials
  • FB news feed stories published for
    • Friend requests
    • New blog posts
    • Comments
  • Easy integration with CE apps (~20 minutes)
