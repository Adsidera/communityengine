Add basic mapping and geo-location functionality to your CE app.

Requires CommunityEngine version 1.0.4 or greater

Check out a demo of a bare CE app with GeoLocation installed: CE-GeoLocation Demo App

Features

  • Adds 'Place' model, representing any generic physical location.
  • Users can be associated with many places (i.e. "My Places")
  • Easy integration with CE apps (~20 minutes)
